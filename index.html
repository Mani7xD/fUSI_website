Welcome to the Future of Neuroimaging

Functional Ultrasound Imaging (fUSI) is an emerging, high-resolution neuroimaging technique that allows researchers and clinicians to observe brain activity in real time by measuring microvascular blood flow. Unlike conventional imaging methods, fUSI is portable, cost-effective, and highly sensitive to dynamic changes in cerebral hemodynamics.

fUSI harnesses ultrafast Doppler imaging to capture neural activity indirectly, leveraging the principle of neurovascular coupling. This makes it an ideal solution for preclinical neuroscience research, neonatal brain monitoring, and even advanced brain-computer interface development.

Our website offers a deep dive into the workings, principles, real-world applications, and latest advancements in fUSI technology. Whether you're a student, scientist, or just curious, you'll find clear explanations, visuals, and helpful links to cutting-edge research.
